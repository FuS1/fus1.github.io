About

Through games, 18Light embarks with you on a long adventure — exploring, creating, and weaving a story that belongs only to us.

In 2012, a group of young dreamers sharing a passion for games came together as a team in a shared apartment on the 18th floor. Three years later, after winning multiple awards with Magnesia, the team officially founded 18Light Game Ltd., and went on to independently develop Behind the Screen — a narrative-driven title recognized by both domestic and international awards, including the IMGA SEA.

The company’s name, “18Light” is a play on words inspired by “spotlight,” embodying our core philosophy: Spotlight Yourself. We are dedicated to creating high-quality games that focus on players. We believe that the most unique part of every game lies in the memories crafted by the players themselves — and our mission is to craft experiences that combine fun and emotional connection.
